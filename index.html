The earth, our planet, a beautiful but catastrophic place. in this pages you will see the most common catastrophes that can happen either in your city or in the rest of the world, log in to receive more information about when, how and where these natural catastrophes are going to happen.

some information of catastrophes

Hurricanes are dangerous and destructive. Also known as cyclones or typhoons in other parts of the world, hurricanes cause high winds, floods, torrential rains, and storm surges (huge waves).

Tsunamis are giant waves caused by earthquakes or volcanic eruptions under the sea. Out in the depths of the ocean, tsunami waves do not dramatically increase in height. But as the waves travel inland, they build up to higher and higher heights as the depth of the ocean decreases.

An earthquake (also known as a quake, tremor or temblor) is the shaking of the surface of the Earth resulting from a sudden release of energy in the Earth's lithosphere that creates seismic waves. Earthquakes can range in size from mild, cannot be felt to violents enough to propel objects and people into the air, and wreak destruction across entire cities.

Tornadoes come in many shapes and sizes, and they are often visible in the form of a condensation funnel originating from the base of a cumulonimbus cloud, with a cloud of rotating debris and dust beneath it. Most tornadoes have wind speeds less than 110 miles per hour (180 km/h), are about 250 feet (80 m) across, and travel a few miles (several kilometers) before dissipating.

Natural disasters are not a joke and we all have to be well informed, either how to take refuge or what to do to avoid having a bad time. On this page we are going to provide you with all the information you need to be prepared for some of these natural catastrophes.
